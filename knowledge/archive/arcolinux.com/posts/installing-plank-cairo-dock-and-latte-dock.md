# Installing plank, cairo-dock and latte-dock

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/plank.jpg)

In this video we will install 

  * plank
  * cairo-dock
  * latte-dock



We will learn that latte-dock works fine on Plasma but not on Xfce.

You can autostart these applications with our ArcoLinux Tweak Tool or remove other autostarting applications.

We install Plasma on our ArcoLinux Xfce, Openbox and i3 system with the ArcoLinux Tweak Tool. Then we see that latte is working just fine.

We change our design in Plasma to have the same look as on our ArcoLinuxB Plasma.
