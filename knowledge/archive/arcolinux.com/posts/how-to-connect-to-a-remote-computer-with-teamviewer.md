# How to connect to a remote computer with teamviewer

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/teamviewer.jpg)

One of the goals of teamviewer is to give people the ability to remotely manage a computer system.

Windows, Mac and Linux alike. It does not matter. 

You can also manage your computer from your ipad or your iphone etc...

**How do we install it on ArcoLinux?**
    
    
    sudo pacman -S arcolinux-teamviewer

That will make sure you have all the dependencies in and that all the services are started.

Then your recipient needs to install teamviewer also on his Windows, Mac or Linus system.

You ask him the **id** and the **password** and you connect.

After logging in you can manage his computer remotely.
