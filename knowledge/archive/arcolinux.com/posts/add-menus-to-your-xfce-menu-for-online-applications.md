# Add menu's to your xfce menu for online applications

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/menu-netflix.jpg)

There are a lot of applications that are hosted online.

  * Netflix.com
  * Pixlr.com
  * Google drive
  * Onedrive
  * Microsoft Word, Excel, Powerpoint online
  * Google Calendar
  * Google Contacts
  * Google Mail
  * and so many more



If you want to have a **menu in your Xfce menu** to those websites then you can easily do this.

Follow the video to know how. We use netflix.com as example.

Remember that in order to play Netflix in Chromium you need to install an extra plugin
    
    
    trizen chromium-widevine
