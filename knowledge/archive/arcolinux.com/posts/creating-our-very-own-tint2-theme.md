# Creating our very own tint2 theme

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ArcoLinux_2018-03-08-45_3840x1080.jpg)   


In this video tutorial we will create a new panel theme in tint2.

Maximize the window of tint2 so you see all the possibilities at once or use the slider to the right.

**Change one element, apply and analyze and remember what you changed. There is no CTRL + Z.**

When your theme is finished we save the theme.

Go to ~/.config/tint2 and copy/paste tint2rc and rename it to **arcolinux-minimal-white.tint2rc** in our example.

This theme will be now added to the ArcoLinux package.
