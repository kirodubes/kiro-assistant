# How to change the wallpaper with nitrogen

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/nitrogen.jpg)

Nitrogen is a straight forward application to **set** your wallpaper. It will however **not** **fetch** **any** new **wallpapers** like variety does. **AND** it will **not** work on **Xfce**.

**ONLY FOR OPENBOX AND I3**

In **preferences** you can add more folders with your personal wallpapers. They will be added to the selection.

If you are on a **dual** screen, you have to tell nitrogen to either go **full** screen (you need to use a dual screen wallpaper then) or decide to put it on **screen 1** or **screen 2**. You can have a different wallpaper per screen.

You have also the standard settings like zoomed fill etc.

And **Variety** will work an all **three** desktop environments.

Keyboard Shortcut To Remember

Control + ALT + I

Launch NITROGEN
