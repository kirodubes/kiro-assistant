# How to get to the bios via Windows 10

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-w10-bios-5.jpg)   


THIS ALSO APPLIES TO WINDOWS 8.

You will have a keyboard shortcut on your system to get in the bios. Normally you can see it when your boot. The latest pc's do not show it anymore or they boot up so fast there is no chance to see it.

Google for **keyboard shortcut** bios and your pc name to find out what keyboard shortcut you have.

Possible keys : Escape, F2, F8, F12 with or without a Fn key

But we can also go via **W10** to this setup utility. Go to Windows setting (Press Windows + I) you can find the **update & security** menu.

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-w10-bios-1.jpg)   


Find **recovery** and click on**Restart now.**

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-w10-bios-2.jpg)   


Then choose the option **Troubleshoot**

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-w10-bios-3.jpg)   


Click on **advanced options**.

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-w10-bios-4.jpg)   


Now you can choose **UEFI** **firmware** **settings**.

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-w10-bios-5.jpg)   


Choose to restart and you will be in the BIOS utility.

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-w10-bios-6.jpg)
