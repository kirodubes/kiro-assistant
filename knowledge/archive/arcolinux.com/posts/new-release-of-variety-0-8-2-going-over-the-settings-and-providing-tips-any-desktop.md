# OLD SETTINGS OF VARIETY

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/01/old-settings-variety.jpg)

Variety received a new version 0.8.2 on 9th of January 2020.

We have updated the configuration of Variety and show you the standard new settings and give you tips.

Use the keyboard shortcuts to save wallpapers or to trash them.

ALT + F or ALT + T

Use the keyboard shortcuts to have the next wallpaper or the previous.

ALT + N or ALT + P

> There are more desktops to be discovered.  
> They are on all our desktops.
