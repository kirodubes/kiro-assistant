# How to install and use zoom to connect to people

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/03/zoom.jpg)

[Zoom](<https://zoom.us>) is one of the tools out there to communicate. You may have seen the last video conference with [Big Daddy Linux](<https://youtu.be/3-oIErxyaSk>). That is great example how this tool can be used professionally.

_Zoom is the leader in modern enterprise video communications, with an easy, reliable cloud platform for video and audio conferencing, chat, and webinars ..._

More and more universities will look for tools to get into contact with their students, companies will want to meet with their employees.

Zoom can be of great assistance.

How to install zoom on ArcoLinux?
    
    
    sudo pacman -S zoom

In the video we set up my headphone and microphone. We take a quick look at all possible settings and we end with a webcam conference with a friend.
