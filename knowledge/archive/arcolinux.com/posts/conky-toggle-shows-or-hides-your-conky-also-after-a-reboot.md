# Conky-toggle shows or hides your conky - also after a reboot

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/conky-toggle.jpg)

**Conky-toggle** is a script that basically quickly **shows** your conky or **hides** your conky. It works together with a .desktop file in your ~/.config/autostart.

When you update the conkies will come into **/etc/skel**. You need to see how you get the new conkies in. We do this with the command skel but there are other ways to update your system. [Check out this article.](<https://arcolinux.com/updating-your-system-with-skel-and-still-keeping-your-own-settings/>)

Some of our desktops have a keyboard shortcut but not all. We will add the shortcut gradually to the others.

Make one yourself on Super + c to am-conky-session and you are golden.
