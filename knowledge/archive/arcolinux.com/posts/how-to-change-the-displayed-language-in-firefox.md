# How to change the displayed language in firefox

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/fiefox.jpg)![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/russian.gif)

You can always change the displayed language of your firefox browser.

We show you in the video and in this animated gif how it is done.

I found out that deleting the extra language is done super quick and efficiently when you delete the language files themselves from your ~/.mozilla folder . The folder will be called differently but the language extension will be in there.
