# Starting from ArcoLinux 18.10 ArcoLinux will support touch-sensitive displays

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/touch.jpg) Starting from ArcoLinux 18.10 we added a package to support all the **portables** with their **touch sensitive screens**. For your information the package is called **xf86-input-elographics**
