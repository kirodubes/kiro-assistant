# Removing ArcoLinux from Windows 11

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/11/removearcolinux.jpg)

Experiment at your own risk.

I learn by trying things out. What ifs and common sense and less reading and watching videos.

Go inside /boot and become root in thunar.

Then delete our arcolinux folder in order to NEVER EVER start up with ArcoLinux anymore.
    
    
    /boot/efi/EFI/

Then you can go to Windows and remove the Linux partition and extend the Windows partition till the end.
