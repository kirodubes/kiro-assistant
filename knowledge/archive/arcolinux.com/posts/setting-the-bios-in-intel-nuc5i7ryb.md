# Setting the BIOS in Intel NUC5i7RYB

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-bios-intel-nuc5i7RYB.jpg)   


We investigate how to get inside the bios of this Intel NUC computer. It might be a different in every computer. I think it is [this](<https://www.intel.com/content/dam/support/us/en/documents/boardsandkits/NUC5i7RYB_NUC5i7RYBR_TechProdSpec.pdf>) computer. ArcoLinux works fine on it.

In our case **F2** is what we need to get into the bios.   
There is also F7 to update BIOS and F10 to Enter Boot Menu.

We check 2 things:

  * can we boot from usb
  * is virtualization enabled to be able to have virtualbox later

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-bios-intel-nuc5i7RYB-intel-virtualization.jpg)   


What you see beneath is the **bootmenu** **F10**. That is needed if you have your usb plugged in and want to boot from USB.

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-bios-intel-nuc5i7RYB-bootmenu.jpg)
