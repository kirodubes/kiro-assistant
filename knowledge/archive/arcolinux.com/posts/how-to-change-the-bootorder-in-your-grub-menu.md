# How to change the bootorder in your grub menu

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/arcolinux-boot-grub-w10.png) My advice is NOT to mix operating systems on one harddisk. Just to avoid frustrations for you and for me. I have a desktop and can easily switch my ssd's to have ArcoLinuxD Cinnamon to ArcoLinuxD Awesome etcetera. But sometimes you have no choice then to put two operating systems on one harddrive. **Let me give you a tip.** **Grubcustomizer** is standard installed on your ArcoLinux. **Grubcustomizer** is not really there to [change colors or give you a nice wallpaper when booting shown here.](<https://arcolinux.com/how-to-change-your-grub-theme/>) **Grubcustomizer** is there to easily **change the boot order when dual booting**. 

> To set Windows 10 back as the default OS to boot into.
