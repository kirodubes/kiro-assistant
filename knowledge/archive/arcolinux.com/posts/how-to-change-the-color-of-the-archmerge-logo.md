# How to change the color of the ArchMerge logo

In this tutorial we are going to change the ArchMerge logo from color. You can find the logo in the following places 

  * [online on this page](<https://arcolinux.info/brand-design/>)
  * on your pc on your home folder at ~/.config/conky/images

You will need inkscape and gpick to do it. Follow the tutorial and recolor our logo to match your awesome wallpaper. Remember this keyboard shortcut to get to the **xfce4-appfinder**. We need to start remembering these names for [phase 2](<https://arcolinux.com//tutorials/>). **ALT + F3** ![](https://arcolinux.com//wp-content/uploads/2017/09/archmerge-conky-recolor-before.jpg)![](https://arcolinux.com//wp-content/uploads/2017/09/archmerge-conky-recolor-after.jpg)
