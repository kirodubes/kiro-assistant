# How to delete variety and all its configuration files

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-variety-gone.png)   


This tutorial is about **Variety**. I am going to show how to **deactivate** variety but also how to **delete** the application and remove any and all of its configuration files.

**Procedure to delete variety and all its configurations.**
    
    
    sudo pacman -R variety
    sudo pacman -R arcolinux-variety-git

Manually remove the folder ~/.config/. Look for a folder called variety.  
Press CTRL + H to see hidden folders.
