# How to set up simplescreenrecorder to share your videos on youtube

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-simplescreenrecorder.jpg)   


Simplescreenrecorder has been my standard recording tool for many years and many tutorials. It is reliable and it just works. I do not wish to edit my videos after. No stunning intro's and stunning endings. Just sharing the knowledge as good and simple as I can make it.

Follow the video to see my settings and to learn from my experience on Awesome windows tiling manager. Some of your settings will need to be tweaked depending on what desktop you are and how powerful your computer is.

> Depending on the desktop and your hardware you might still experience slight glitches. Experiment with the settings of the application and see what works with your hardware.
