# Installing and using viber on your ArcoLinux system

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/viber.jpg)   


You check out if you want **viber** by visiting their [website](<https://www.viber.com>).

Installing could not be easier.

Install it first on your mobile (ios or android) - see that you have an account.

Then proceed to ArcoLinux and install with
    
    
    yay -S viber

You will get popups guiding you through the installation. You scan a code with your phone and that makes the link to your ArcoLinux desktop app.

Then you will be able to receive and send information from your desktop.
