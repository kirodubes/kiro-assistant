# Playing Xonotic on ArcoLinux

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/xonotic.jpg)

Have some fun playing this shooter.
    
    
    sudo pacman -S xonotic

Since xonotic is not coming from Aur I could have used the above command.

In the video I used the **yay** command.

This means that IF you do not know where it is coming from **yay** will always know where to get it.
