# Changing sublime-text-dev not to check for an update

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/sublime-text.jpg) When we update our engines today we see a message in sublime-text-dev. **"update_check": false** If you do not want your sublime-text to check for updates, you need to change a setting. That is explained in the video.
