# How to fix the neofetch logo after updating neofetch to version 5

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/06/ArcoLinuxB-Plasma_20180619_215352.png)

After updating neofetch to version 5 our Ascii logo of ArcoLinux seems broken. It is actually a png image translated to ascii.

All we need to do is type a **"#"** on the right place **OR** **wait for the update** to come in and copy/paste it from **/etc/skel/.config/neofetch/config.conf** to your own directory structure. 

In the meantime the configuration file has been renamed by Neofetch and we follow.  
it will differ from the video.

The **current** configuration file is **config.conf.** You can **delete** the **config** and the **config-backup** file.
