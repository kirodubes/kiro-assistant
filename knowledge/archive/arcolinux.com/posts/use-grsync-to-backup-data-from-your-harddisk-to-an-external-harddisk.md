# Use grsync to backup data from your harddisk to an external harddisk

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/grsync.jpg)   


**Grsync** is installed by default on ArcoLinux. 

It is the front-end or **gtk** **gui** for **rsync** , which is a command you can use in the terminal.
