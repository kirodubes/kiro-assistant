# Use imgur to share pictures with us on our forum

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/imgur.jpg)   


To give you support with ArcoLinux you can take pictures of your system and upload them to [imgur.com](<https://imgur.com/>).

If the image is too big, you can make it a bit smaller or you can give us a link to the original image or you can choose a smaller size in imgur. See picture.
