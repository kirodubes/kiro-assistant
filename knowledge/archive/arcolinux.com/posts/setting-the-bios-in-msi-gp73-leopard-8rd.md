# SETTING THE BIOS IN MSI GP73 LEOPARD 8RD

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/09/msileopard.jpg)   


We investigate how to get inside the bios of this MSI laptop. It might be a different in every computer. Here you can find more info on the [laptop](<https://www.msi.com/Laptop/GP63-Leopard-8RD.html>). [  
](<https://www.intel.com/content/dam/support/us/en/documents/boardsandkits/NUC5i7RYB_NUC5i7RYBR_TechProdSpec.pdf>)

ArcoLinux works fine on it.

In our case **DELETE** is what we need to know get into the **BIOS**. Keep pressing it when your system launches.

There is also **F11** to enter the **Boot Menu**.

We check 2 things:

  * secure boot disabled?
  * is virtualization enabled to be able to have virtualbox later
