# Use peek to show us what goes wrong on your system

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/peek.jpg)

If you want to explain something in order to get help there is this application **peek**.

It will generate an **animated** **gif** you can then upload to [imgur](<https://imgur.com>) and put the link in the forum.

Working in this manner we can see what is going on on your end.
