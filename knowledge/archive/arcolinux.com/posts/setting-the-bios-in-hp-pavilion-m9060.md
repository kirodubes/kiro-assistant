# Setting the bios in Hp Pavilion m9060

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-virtualization-technology-bios.jpg) We investigate how to get inside the bios of this HP computer. It might be a different in every computer. I think it is this computer more or less. I do think it was m9060... It is an 8-9 year old computer. ArcoLinux works fine on it. In our case ESC is what we need to get into the bios. We check 2 things: 

  * can we boot from usb
  * is virtualization enabled to be able to have virtualbox later

What you see beneath is the bootmenu **ESC**. That is needed if you have your usb plugged in and want to boot from USB. ![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-bios-hp-pavilion.jpg)
