# How to set up variety with the use of desktoppr and dropbox

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/09/variety.jpg)

This tutorial is about **Variety**. I am going to show my personal settings and how I use **desktoppr.co** to improve my workflow to keep the best wallpapers in one place i.e. **dropbox**.

Get yourself a **dropbox** account. [Installing dropbox is explained in this article](<https://arcolinux.com//how-to-install-dropbox-and-how-to-use-it/>).

Then create an account on desktoppr.co.

Afterwards you can just click on the **cloud** icon and you will save that wallpaper to your dropbox folder. But any wallpaper you put in that folder will also be synced to desktoppr.co. It goes in two ways. Sharing is caring. All my wallpapers are on here for anyone to use.

We go over the **settings** of variety and see where my wallpapers are.
