# How to share you hardware specifications when asking for support

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/arcolinux-xfce-settings-quick-system-info.jpg) We often do **not get enough** **information** to be able to analyze the issue. If you think we need to know your **hardware** **specifications** to solve your issue, you have now since ArcoLinux 6.8.1 an extra icon in your Xfce settings. **Quick System Info** will give you the information about your hardware. Make a screenshot with CTRL + PrtScr. Save it and upload it imgur.com. Use the forum code to copy/paste it in your post on the forum. That will speed things up. You can give the same information typing this command in your terminal. 
    
    
    inxi -F
