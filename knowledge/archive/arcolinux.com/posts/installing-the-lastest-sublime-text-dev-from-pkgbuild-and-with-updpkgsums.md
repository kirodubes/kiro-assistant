# Installing the lastest Sublime-Text-Dev from PKGBUILD and with updpkgsums

![](https://arcolinux.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/sublime-text-dev.jpg)

THERE IS NO NEED TO INSTALL THE LATEST VERSION

Let us assume that having the last version of Sublime-Text-Dev is important to you and you can not wait an other day till the maintainer has updated the PKGBUILD. Your update will come in automatically by that time.

We will show you why **sublime** -**text** is an important **utility**. It is one of the editors that will ask your root password, if you edit files that are normally protected.

> Sublime-text is great for your workflow.

We will show you

  * how and where to change the setting to **autologin**
  * how and where to change what **desktop** you log into by default



Furthermore you will see a great tip and tool **updpkgsums** to make it faster to build/update your PKGBUILD.
