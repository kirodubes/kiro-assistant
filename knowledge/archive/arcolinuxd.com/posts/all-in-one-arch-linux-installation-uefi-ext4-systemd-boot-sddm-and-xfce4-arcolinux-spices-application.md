In this all-in-one video we will put the emphasis on the boot procedure.

You can choose many ways to boot your Arch Linux system.

[Read about them on the Arch wiki.](<https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Arch_boot_process>)

[We choose systemd-boot to boot up our system.](<https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Systemd-boot>)

We create three files or ways to boot up:

  * default
  * fallback
  * go to TTY
