# 9 Change logo and many settings in Mate menu

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/mate-menu.jpg) This time we **do not need to install** dconf-editor to change the settings of this menu. You can however still change them there too. 
    
    
    sudo pacman -S dconf-editor

With a right-mouse click on the menu anything can be changed not only the icon. ![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/archmerged-mate-menu-preferences.jpg)
