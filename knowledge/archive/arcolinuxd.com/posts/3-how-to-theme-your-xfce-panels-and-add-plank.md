# 3 - How to theme your xfce panels and add plank

When you install Xfce you will get the standard menu, the panel will be at the top and you will have a panel at the bottom from Xfce with icons. That is NOT plank.

> Linux is about freedom. 

You do not have to copy me. I am just showing what possibilities you have. 

We move the **panel** from top to bottom. We check out what **settings** of the panel we can change like transparency.

We **delete** the bottom panel and replace it with our plank and all its themes.

We change the standard menu with the **whisker** menu. It has LOADS of settings to tweak even further.
