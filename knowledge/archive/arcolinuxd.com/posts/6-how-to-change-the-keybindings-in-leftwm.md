# 6 How to change the keybindings in Leftwm

In this short video we show you that sxhkd and the configs of each Tiling Window Manager will be behind the keybindings.

You can change them if you want. You need them to keep safe under an other name and copy/paste them back if you do an accidental "skel".

The bulk of the keybindings are in **sxhkdrc** but there are also keybindings in the **config.toml**.  
If you start changing them, remeber that you can not have doubles are else it will not work.
