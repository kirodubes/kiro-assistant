# 6 Choose the display option on a dual screen system on plasma

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/displaychoose.jpeg)![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/switch-screen.gif)

It is great that Plasma immediately recognizes the fact that it has a dual screen.  
We see this choice when you boot up. Check out the video or this bmp that shows the options. There is a variety of choices for your system.

You can invoke this window by pressing

> Super + P

Video: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sZo2uiM_LV8>
