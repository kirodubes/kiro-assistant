# 3 Update your system and check your /etc/skel - ANY DESKTOP

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/05/arcolinuxd-bspwm-upall.jpg)

ArcoLinux is a rolling release. 

Some of the updates will be installed in **/usr/share**. Nothing to do then.  
Some of the updates will be installed in **/etc/skel**. The user decides whether he wants to take action or not.
    
    
    sudo pacman -Syyu

or
    
    
    upall

There was a message about **/etc/skel** during our update and we show you how to update anything being installed in /etc/skel.
