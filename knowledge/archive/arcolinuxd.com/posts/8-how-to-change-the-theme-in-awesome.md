# 8 How to change the theme in Awesome

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/arcolinux-awesome-theme.jpg)

We will use atom (and extra packages : lua syntax, pigments and minimap) to change the themes.

Search for the word "theme" and change the theme by changing the number.

We changed the weather to New York in the blue theme. Changing the theme to "2" means we have the weather back from Antwerp.

We will investigate what differences there are with **meld**. And we put the city id back into the second theme.
