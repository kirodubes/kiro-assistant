# 4 Changing your desktop preferences in LXQt

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/desktop-preferences.jpg)

We can change our desktop preferences in LXQt.

There is are a lot of parameters we can set. In the video we take a look at backgrounds, icons, label text.

ArcoLinux has created a wallpaper package for people who have dual screens.

In the second tab called "Slide Show" you can enable it. We have filled in the pathway to that particular package and you will get one big wallpaper stretched over your dual screen.

In the advanced tab you can add even more icons on your desktop like Home, Trash, Computer and Network.
