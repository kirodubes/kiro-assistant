# Use asciinema to show what goes on in your terminal

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/asciinema.jpg) In order to give you better support we need to know what is going on your terminal. This will be great if you are on **ArcoLinuxD**. Then we do not have a graphical interface. **Asciinema** is going to **record** what is happening in your **terminal**. 
    
    
    asciinema rec

Type exit to stop recording. Then you will have a link to share what is going on your machine. Use the help to learn more about it asciinema -h Just an example. https://asciinema.org/a/yrsfoD1vGfSqBt2KUimvzfc3p
