# 6 Qtile will use compton as compositor

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/arcolinuxb-qtile-2.jpg)

compton.conf

 

Compton has 3 big parts in its file.

  * Shadow
  * Opacity
  * Fading



We have an [Arch Wiki page](<https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/Compton>) to learn even more.

Choose **glx** or **xrender**. Only if you have trouble with your graphical hardware.

We show you that there are lots of articles and video's about compton.

Compton is a project that is under constant development and we just follow along.
