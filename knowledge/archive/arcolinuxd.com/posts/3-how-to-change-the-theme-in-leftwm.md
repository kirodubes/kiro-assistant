# 3 How to change the theme in Leftwm

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/smooth.jpg)

Leftwm is ideal to change or create themes.

We have an application called leftwm-theme that will assist you.

Three steps to remember:

  1. leftwm-theme update
  2. leftwm-theme install ...
  3. leftwm-theme apply ...



We will only maintain candy and smooth themes. These are from ArcoLinux. The others are from the **community**.

> Community themes may work or they may not work
