# 12 How to autostart plank in ArcoLinuxD Mate

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/archmerged-mate-plank.jpg) In most desktops you can start applications at startup. They provide you with a gui or graphical interface that will guide you through the process of adding an application or service to the "autostart" list. But in essence they will use the hidden folder ~/.config/autostart and put a .desktop file to start an application when you log into your display manager like lightdm. Follow the video to see how.
