# 8 Activating the conkies of ArcoLinux on LXQt

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/conky.png)

We can always use any of our conkies on LXQt. 

Conkyzen is available to pick any of them by hand.

We just move through the list via CTRL + ALT + Pagedown or Pageup.

Some of them will look nice - others will not. That is normal. Every desktop has its own window manager and they treat a conky differently. If you like a conky that does not look good, you can manually fix it by editing the parameters of the conky.
