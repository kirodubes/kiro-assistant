# 24 Plasma seems to autostart applications upon login like a previous session

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/autostart-session-at-login.jpeg)

After making all these tutorials it sometimes happened that simplescreenrecorder was started at login. 

**Plasma will standard remember the apps in your last session and will reload them when you log back in.**

Where can we tell Plasma to start with a clean session aka no apps from a previous setting?

You can see the setting in the picture and in the video.

Video: <https://youtu.be/X1THpT3OZEg>
