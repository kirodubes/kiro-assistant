# Help yourself and use links to look up information on the net in the TTY

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/arcolinux-links.jpg) When you are installing ArcoLinuxD you start with a TTY screen. It is all black and you type what you want to accomplish. I wanted to get rid of the 'ugly' shutdown lines and I knew what to do but wondered what the exact code was again. You can install links: 
    
    
    sudo pacman -S links

And then **surf** with links to https://arcolinux.com and search for **shutdown** to find the article with the answer and that in the TTY. 

> This was assuming you have only one pc and no smartphone. How do you find your answer then?
