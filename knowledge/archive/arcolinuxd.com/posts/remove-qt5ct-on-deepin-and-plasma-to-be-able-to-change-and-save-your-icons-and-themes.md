# Remove qt5ct on Deepin and Plasma to be able to change and save your icons and themes

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/deepin.jpg)

Check if you have qt5ct installed in Plasma or Deepin. That application will overrule your designated theme and icon changer.
    
    
    sudo pacman -R qt5ct
