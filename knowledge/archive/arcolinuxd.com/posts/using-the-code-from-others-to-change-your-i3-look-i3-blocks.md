# 20 Using the code from others to change your i3 look - i3 blocks

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/najib-bennassi.jpg)

You can find many **configuration** files or dot files out there on the net for **i3**.

You can use some of these files to improve your i3 settings.

Google : "github i3 config" and you will find dozens of people sharing their config.

That is how you learn by reading other people's config and taking what you like and want and discarding what you do not like.

In this case one of our user **Najib** **Bennani** has changed the original i3blocks to become more **colorful**.

I will show you what to do if you like his setup. It is the procedure that is important. You can do similar actions with other githubs out there.

We will use his configuration on [github](<https://github.com/najibni/NUC-Archi3>).
