# 6 Changing the logo of Neofetch - any desktop

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/arcolinux-awesome-termite-logo-white.jpg)![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/arcolinux-awesome-neofetch-image.jpg)Check the manual of any application. In this case 
    
    
    man neofetch

We need to tell neofetch to use the option **\- -source** to load another image or logo. We get our ArcoLinux logo's with the package from the conky's : arcolinux-conky-collection-git. It is installed in /etc/skel. We move it to our own home folder.  

W3m and imagemagick need to be installed to have the logo of ArcoLinux in Neofetch
