# 2 How to change icons, themes and cursors in ArcoLinuxD Gnome

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/archmerged-gnome-icons-themes-cursors.jpg) In Gnome we need an extra package called **gnome-tweak-tool** to change icons, themes, cursors and much more. We use the **Activities** in the top left to get an overview of all your applications and windows that are open. You can start typing to look for an application. You can activate this also by pressing **Super**. For the i3 users I installed also dmenu in this desktop. Activate it with Super + Shift + D
