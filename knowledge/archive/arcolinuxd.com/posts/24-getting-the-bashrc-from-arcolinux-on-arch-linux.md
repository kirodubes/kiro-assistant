# 24 getting the bashrc from ArcoLinux on Arch Linux

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/bashrc.jpg)

What if you want to re-use the .bashrc of ArcoLinux on Arch Linux.

**Option 1 :**

Add the repositories of ArcoLinux to Arch Linux. [See this article.](<https://www.arcolinuxd.com/how-to-add-spices-to-arch-linux-xfce-conkies-bashrc/>)

**Option 2:**

I just need the .bashrc nothing more. [Follow this link](<https://github.com/arcolinux/arcolinux-root/blob/master/etc/skel/.bashrc-latest>).

Now copy/paste the contents of that file in your .bashrc in your home directory.

Reboot or log out.

Install any of the missing applications we use like reflector, expac, ...
