# 18 Replacing the icons in the qtile menu with other icons

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/05/arcolinuxb-qtile-awesome-cheatsheet.jpg)

We have chosen to take icons for the workplaces.

Icons can mean anything and they can easily be replaced with a copy/paste from [the Awesome Font website](<https://fontawesome.com/v5/cheatsheet>).

You see a better icon.

Just copy/paste it in the config.py.

Make sure to make a copy of your files.

The **Skel** command will overwrite your config.
