# 8 How to add shortcuts to your preferred applications on Plasma

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/all-shortcuts-vivaldi.jpeg)

With a right mouse click on any application in your menu you will have 4 options

  1. add shortcut to the desktop
  2. add shortcut to the panel as widget
  3. pin to taskmanager
  4. add to favorites



Enough choice.

You decide what works for you.

The video will show how to make them **but also how to delete them again**.

Video: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8YlfFNNwtTU>
