# 6 Setting up our cloud services dropbox and google drive - any desktop

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/arcolinuxb-deepin-dropbox-google-drive.jpg)

My personal way to keep save from data loss is keeping my data in the cloud. There are people who copy/paste (grsync) everything to a harddisk or usb. There are people who use timeshift and other tools.

At some point in your life you find a workflow that fits you. This is mine. 

**All the data is stored in the cloud** and after every clean installation I reconnect to the cloud and I decide what data I want to get in on that particular ssd. We can always sync selectively in both.

Then I let the syncing to its work and I can start working.

We see in the video that I connect to **dropbox** and google drive via the application **insync**.
