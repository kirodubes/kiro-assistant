# 11 How to change to a dual monitor setup on herbstluftwm

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/hlwm-dual-monitor.jpg)

In this video we use **xrandr** and **arandr** to analyze what we need to do to get Herbstluftwm to use both of my monitor aka a dual monitor setup.

We also change the **qwerty** setting to **azerty** setting and update our system with **update** , **skel** , **cb** and **upall**.
