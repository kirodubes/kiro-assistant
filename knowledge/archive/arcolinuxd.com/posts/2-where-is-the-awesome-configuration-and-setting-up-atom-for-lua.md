# 2 Where is the Awesome configuration and setting up Atom for Lua

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/arcolinux-awesome-configuration-files.jpg)

Every desktop has its own "headquarters". A folder, where all the settings reside...
    
    
    ~/.config/awesome

With **Super + S** you get an overview of all the shortcuts that are available. Do not like the shortcuts. Change them.

We talk about the different folders and files that are part of Awesome.

**rc.lua** is the essence of your awesome configuration file.

We learn you to open the lua files with **Atom.** It has some nice packages to use **color syntax** for **lua**.

At the bottom we start the **autostart.sh** and **compton**.

The updated Awesome will come to **/etc/skel**.
