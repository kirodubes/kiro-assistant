# 11 - Setting your dual screen in Xfce

Go to the Xfce **settings** and look for the **display** icon. There you can set that you do not want to mirror your displays and that is all you need to do. You can use this also on **openbox** but **not** on **i3**. ![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/settings-display.png)
