# 48 - using the cloud service of Vivaldi to get settings and bookmarks synced

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/06/vivaldi-cloud.jpg)

Use the cloudservices the browsers provide.

Firefox, chromium, google chrome and also Vivaldi can safe your settings, bookmarks, ... in the cloud.

All you need to do is make an account with a secure password. You can even encrypt the online data.

After a clean install and after logging in, all your data will be synced to your computer.

Use the cloud services to improve your workflow.
