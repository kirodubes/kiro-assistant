# 3 - All about conky's on ArcoLinuxD Cinnamon

There are conky's and **desklets** in Cinnamon. Let us check out the difference. You can find conkyzen in your menu and move it to the favorites panel. Two things to remember 

  * am-conkyzen is our overview of all conky's
  * am-conky-session will run the conky saved in the last conky-sessionfile

We can tell cinnamon to start up our conky **automatically** at **bootup**. New conky's come in all at the time...   **What should we do when a new update of the conky's comes in?** Copy/paste the **/etc/skel/config/conky** folder to your own home directory. We compare it with **meld** to see the differences.
