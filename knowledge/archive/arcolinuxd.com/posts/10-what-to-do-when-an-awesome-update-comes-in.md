# 10 What to do when an Awesome update comes in

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/arcolinux-awesome-update.jpg)

When we update awesome you will get a new package in. It will be saved to **/etc/skel**. We will never save it to your home directory.

We use **meld** to compare what I have and what comes in from ArcoLinux.

**Nitrogen --restore** has been added for people who do not want to use variety but want to use nitrogen as wallpaper setter.

Compare your version with ours and enjoy.

We have set the wallpaper back to starwars.

Then we will use nitrogen rather than variety to set our wallpapers.

 

# Remember to logout to set nitrogen to work.
