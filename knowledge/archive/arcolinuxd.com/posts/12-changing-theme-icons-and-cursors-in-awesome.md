# 12 Changing theme, icons and cursors in Awesome

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/arcolinux-awesome-lxappearance.jpg)

We will use lxappearance to change the theme, icons and cursors.

In the menu you can find it in "customize look and feel".

I rather like to use rofi with Super +F12 and choose 'lxappearance'.

We will change our wallpaper on the basis of a pink wallpaper that comes in from variety.

Choose an icon theme.
