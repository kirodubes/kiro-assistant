# 13 How to get wobbly windows on Plasma

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/wobbly-windows-plasma.jpeg)

## IF your hardware can not perform the   
wobbly windows  
it will NOT show up in your selection.

There is a filter at the top right. That is how I found out on one of my computers that wobbly windows was not supported.

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/notsupported.jpeg)

One of the things I missed when working on this new Plasma setup challenge is one of the desktop effects. You might have seen it in compiz in other video's.

> WOBBLY WINDOWS

**There is no need to install anything. It just works out of the box on the compositor of Plasma. You just need to activate it.**

The picture above and the video show you how to activate the setting.   
We will record from now on with this desktop effect on. You will see its effect all the time.

Video: <https://youtu.be/Lcu2IawAG2s>
