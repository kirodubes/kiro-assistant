# 9 Exploring the possibilities in deepin filemanager

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/arcolinuxb-deepin-filemanager.jpg)

TIP : READ THE HELP OF DEEPIN file manager

The filemanager is called **dde-filemanager**. Use the tab in the terminal to learn all the applications of a desktop.

Type **deepin** in the terminal and press twice on TAB.  
Type **dde** in the terminal and press twice on TAB.

And you can learn a lot about your desktop that way.

**CTRL + H does work** \- hide of unhide the hidden files/folders will work - we will show you why it is not working on your first window when opening the filemanager.

You can set Deepin to a black theme and so much more.

Follow the video to learn more.

Video: <https://youtu.be/i3t0ObyeRiI>

There is even a preview available for video's. Images, texts, documents, compressed files. They can all get a preview.

Just **select** a video and press **spacebar** and you can watch a preview and quickly scan through it.

Video: <https://youtu.be/KJYTJTtpn0Y>
