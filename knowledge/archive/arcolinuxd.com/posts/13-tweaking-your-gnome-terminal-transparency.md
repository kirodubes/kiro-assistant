# 13 Tweaking your gnome terminal - transparency

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/archmerged-gnome-terminal-transparancy.jpg)

Gnome terminal can be changed and tweaked any way you see fit.

We make sure the menubar is gone, bigger fonts, transparent background, no scrollbar and limit scrollback is gone.

We installed an AUR package via the scripts called

**gnome-terminal-transparency**

**That is why we can have a transparent background.**
