# 1 Plasma challenge of November 2018

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/all-shortcuts-vivaldi.jpeg)

**November 2018** we started making tutorials about Plasma and launched the **Plasma Challenge**.

Plasma can be installed with the **BYOI** script from **ArcoLinuxB**.

The **Plasma challenge of November 2018** was based on an ISO from ArcoLinuxB Plasma and we added all the games for some fun time. Like **Sauerbraten** , **Xonotic** , **Steam** and **Lutris**.

Tutorial how to build your own ISO is here :[ https://arcolinuxb.com/byoi-on-arcolinux-plasma/](<https://arcolinuxb.com/byoi-on-arcolinux-plasma/>)

or 

you can **download** the ISO from [**Sourceforge**.](<https://sourceforge.net/projects/arcolinux-community-editions/files/plasma/>) See also announcement underneath.

Video: <https://youtu.be/zV2pQ9wMyvE>

## **Plasma Challenge**

You can follow our tutorials based on either of two ISO's.

**1\. ArcoLinuxB Plasma (recommended)**

We record the plasma video's on an SSD installed from the **ArcoLinuxB Plasma ISO**.  
You can either **create** your very own ArcoLinuxB Plasma ISO [following this tutorial](<https://arcolinuxb.com/byoi-on-arcolinux-plasma/>) or [download it from Sourceforge](<https://sourceforge.net/projects/arcolinux-community-editions/files/plasma/>). (ArcoLinuxB-plasma-18.11.2.ISO of 4.3 GB - games included)

**2\. ArcoLinuxD Plasma**

You can also use the ArcoLinuxD ISO and then install Plasma with the help of the github scripts. This will not be an exact copy of the ISO.

> Learn about the Plasma desktop and enjoy it.
