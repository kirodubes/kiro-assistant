# 11 Single click or double click to open files and folders in Plasma

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/11/workspacebehavior.jpeg)

This was surely one of the nuisances I experienced at first on Plasma. 

Why? 

Because we were accustomed to another workflow on the other desktops. That is all it is. A personal preference.

If you want to able to click a file or folder to be able to select it and delete it for example then you need to change a setting.

The image above and the video below show you what setting needs to be changed.

You can keep the setting to **single-click to open files and folders** and use also **CTRL** or **SHIFT** to select the files or folders with your mouse WITHOUT opening it.

Video: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aO43KhOdO9E>
