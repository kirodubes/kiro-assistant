# 12 changing the appearance of deepin - icons, themes, cursors, ... or personalisation of Deepin

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/02/arcolinuxb-deepin-personalisation.jpg)

We navigate to the control center and take a look at **personalisation.**

In theme you will find the

  * theme
  * icons
  * cursors



We select **Arc Dark**. You will see that not all applications will follow this theme. That is normal.

**Thunar** will now follow the **Arc Dark** theme but **not** the filemanager of Deepin for example.

We have lots of icons because of the scripts we ran in the nemesis script. [See previous article for more info](<https://www.arcolinuxd.com/4-installing-arcolinux-nemesis-on-any-desktop-as-a-possible-workflow-after-installation/>).

You can set the **transparency** of the control center.

You can change the **font** and the **size** of the fonts.

Last but not least you can enable or disable the **window** **effects**. This sometimes does not work on virtualbox. When installing Deepin on vb you will be asked if you want to enable the desktop effects or not.

Better install Deepin on SSD really.
