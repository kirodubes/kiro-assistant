# 4 Overview of all the possible settings in ArcoLinuxD Gnome

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/archmerged-gnome-settings.jpg) We take a look at the settings of Gnome. Add the applications you want to the dash of Gnome. Right mouse click will remove an application from the dash again. Add the settings and gnome-tweak-tool to your dash you will need it. We go over all settings. Bear in mind that this video is created on a **desktop** and **not** a **laptop**. No wifi, no battery, etc. Sound tool is present. I have **pavucontrol** installed for my bluetooth headset. **Tip : Super + Shift + D is a menu I migrated from i3wm.** **Keyboard** **shortcuts** are hidden away quite deep. Go to **Devices** and Keyboard. **Preferred** **applications** are also hidden away quite deep. Go to **Details** and **Default Applications**.
