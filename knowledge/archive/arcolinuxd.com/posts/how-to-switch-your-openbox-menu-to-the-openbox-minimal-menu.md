# How to switch your Openbox menu to the Openbox Minimal menu

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/arcolinuxb-openbox-minimal2.jpg)![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/arcolinuxb-openbox-minimal-3.jpg)

Thanks to the fact that we have created ArcoLinuxB Openbox Minimal we now have a second kind of menu.

A minimal one.

Once you know your way around the system and know where all the core files are for openbox, you can access them via Thunar and edit them. No need for a menu.

This gives us the possibility to make a 'lighter' menu.
    
    
    sudo pacman -S arcolinux-obmenu-generator-minimal
