# 9 Change the gnome settings with your gnome tweak tool

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/02/archmerged-gnome-tweak-tool.jpg)

Let us take a longer look at the gnome-tweak-tool.

We can change **themes** , **icons** and **cursors** but there is more.

**Desktop** settings are tricky. Do you want to work on your desktop? Have icons on your desktop.

We can change **fonts** \- we change the font to the standard font from ArcoLinux :

**noto sans regular**

Add extra buttons on the titlebar : **maximize** and **minimize**.

And so much more settings - just a selection of the ones I need/like.
