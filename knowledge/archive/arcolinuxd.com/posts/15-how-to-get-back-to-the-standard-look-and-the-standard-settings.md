# 15 How to get back to the standard look and the standard settings

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/11/dwm.jpg)

Nomally we would do a "skel" to get our old configs back.

> In DWM this will not work.

Skel is needed to get the old configs back BUT you need to build it again.

So we "make and sudo make install" dwm and slstatus then we can reboot and you will have the initial setup back.
