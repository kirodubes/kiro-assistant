# 13 What can I do with the mouse in Xmonad

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/arcolinuxb-xmonad-mouse-bindings.jpg)

We can do several things related with the mouse.

With Super + **left** key mouse press you can make a window become **floating** and **move** it where you want.

You **resize** the window with Super + **right** mouse click.

If you want to tile a floating window, you press Ctrl + Shift + t.
