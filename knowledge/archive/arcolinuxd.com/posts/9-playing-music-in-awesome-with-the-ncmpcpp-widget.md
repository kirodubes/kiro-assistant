# 9 Playing music in Awesome with the ncmpcpp widget

![](https://www.arcolinuxd.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/03/ncmpcpp.jpg)

[Ncmpcpp](<https://wiki.archlinux.org/index.php/ncmpcpp>) is a great way to play music from your music library from the terminal. There are lots of articles about ncmpcpp on the websites.

Use the **music** **note** **icon** to **launch** and **stop** ncmpcpp. **Right** **mouse** **click** on the music note stops the application.

There are more shortcuts for ncmpcpp available.

Use Super + S to find them out.

Video: <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t99kpGdanCE>
